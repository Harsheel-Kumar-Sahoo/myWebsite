Practical 1

Program that accepts two integers and prints their sum.

Source Code

# Practical 1
a = int(input('Enter the first integer: '))
b = int(input('Enter the second integer: '))

sum = a + b

print('The two integers are:', a, b)
print('The sum of the given two integers is:', sum)

Output

Enter the first integer: 60
Enter the second integer: 52
The two integers are: 60 52
The sum of the given two integers is: 112

Practical 2

Program that accepts the radius of a circle and prints its area.

Source Code

# Practical 2
r = int(input('Enter the radius of the circle: '))
area = 3.14 * r**2
print('The area of the circle is:', area)

Output

Enter the radius of the circle: 4
The area of the circle is: 50.24

Practical 3

Program that accepts base and height of a triangle and calculate the area of triangle.

Source Code

# Practical 3
b = float(input('Enter the base of the triangle:'))
h = float(input('Enter the height of the triangle:'))
area = (1/2)*b*h
print('The area of triangle is:', area)

Output

Enter the base of the triangle:4.6
Enter the height of the triangle:22
The area of triangle is: 8

Practical 4

Program that accepts a student's marks in three subjects (out of 100) and prints the percentage marks.

Source Code

# Practical 4
print('Enter the marks of three subjects each out of 100.')
a = float(input('Enter the marks of first subject:'))
b = float(input('Enter the marks of second subject:'))
c = float(input('Enter the marks of third subject:'))
P=(a+b+c)/3
print('The percentage marks are:', P, '%')

Output

Enter the marks of three subjects each out of 100.
Enter the marks of first subject:89
Enter the marks of second subject:97
Enter the marks of third subject:94
The percentage marks are:99.33%
